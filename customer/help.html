Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book an appointment?

Click on "Book Appointment" from the dashboard, select a property, choose your preferred date and time, then confirm your booking.

Can I cancel my appointment?

Yes, you can cancel your appointment from the booking details page before it's completed.

How do I leave a review?

After your appointment is completed, you can leave a review from the booking details page or from My Bookings.

Are all agents verified?

Yes, all agents are verified and approved by our admin team before they can list properties.
